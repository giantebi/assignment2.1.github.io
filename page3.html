SNCS Level 1

Sport climbing level 1 in Singapore is a certification course for new climbers intending to take up climbing. The objective of this course is to give participants an enjoyable, safe and informative introduction to sport climbing.

Participants will be introduced to the various climbing equipment being used, climbing techniques, checking procedures, belaying for top-roping techniques and climbing calls. This course is a requirement when belaying in Singapore climbing gyms. It is also recognised in climbing gyms around South East Asia, subject to their respective terms and conditions.

SNCS Level 1 is certified under Singapore Mountaineering Federation.

Requirements

- 13 years old
- No climbing Experience
- Fully Vacinnated


Passing Criterias

- Correct usage of climbing equipment
- 5 step belaying technique
- Basic Climbing Technique
- Pre climb checks
- Variations of Climbing
- Caring and Inspection Tips
- Climbing Commands
- Safety Precautions
- Spotting

Pricing

$70 (1 day course)

SNCS Level 2

Sport climbing level 2 is an advancement from the top-roping skills acquired from Level 1 and progresses into lead climbing on artificial climbing walls. Participants must be confident in BOTH climbing and belaying techniques as covered in Level 1 before taking on this course.

This course is a requirement when doing lead climbing in Singapore climbing gyms. It is also recognised in climbing gyms around South East Asia, subject to their respective terms and conditions.

SNCS Level 2 is certified under Singapore Mountaineering Federation.

Requirements

- Complete SNCS Level 1 certification course
- Able to climb simple route without difficulty (Grade 6A)
- Fully Vacinnated


Passing Criterias

- Lead climbing equipment
- Dynamic belayer technique
- Arrest climber's fall
- Placement of protection points
- Climbing and clipping
- Undo clippings
- Safe falling position
- Cleaning route
- Sport and natural rock wall
- Safety Precautions

Pricing

$140 (2 days course)

SNCS Level 3



Sport climbing level 3 is designed as a follow on from the Sport Climbing Course Level 2 and Abseiling Level 1 or equivalent. This course builds on Lead Climbing Skills to advanced rope skills for safe anchor construction and rope management from suspended belay positions. Participants on this course should be confident in all the techniques covered at Level Two before undertaking this more advanced level course.

SNCS Level 3 is certified under Singapore Mountaineering Federation.

Requirements

- Complete SNCS Level 2 certification course
- Completed SNAS Abseiling Level 1
- Fully Vacinnated


Passing Criterias

- Anchor building Equipment
- Top down belaying technique
- Multi pitch climbing
- Types of protection points
- Rig up for abseil
- Rescue Techniques
- Setting up anchors
- Leading and seconding
- Abseil using backup system
- Accessory cords

Pricing

$300 (2 days course)
