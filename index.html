OUR HISTORY

Climb Central originated in Singapore in 2015. The founders of Climb Central have been associated with rock climbing for the past 20+ years and Climb Central was an effort by the founders to make the sport accessible to all.

Climb Central (Sports Hub) have been endorsed as the main training centre for the Singapore Climbing team.

Since its opening, it have certified more than thousand rock climbers around the world, to equip them with the skills for outdoor climbing.
